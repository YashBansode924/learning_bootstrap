Gana App - (FAQ)

Gana App is a music streaming platform where users can listen to songs, podcasts, and radio in multiple Indian and international languages.

Yes, Gana App offers a free version with ads. You can upgrade to Gana Plus for ad-free music, offline downloads, and premium sound quality.

Gana App supports songs in Hindi, English, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Punjabi, Kannada, Malayalam, Bhojpuri, Gujarati, and many more regional languages.

Yes, offline downloads are available with the Gana Plus subscription, so you can enjoy your favorite music without an internet connection.

Simply log in, search for your favorite songs, and click the "Add to Playlist" option. You can create and manage multiple playlists easily.

Yes, you can use Gana App on Android, iOS smartphones, tablets, and web browsers for a seamless music experience.

You can upgrade to Gana Plus through the app or website by selecting a suitable plan and making the payment via UPI, credit/debit card, or other available options.

Spotify - (FAQ)

Spotify is a global music streaming platform that allows users to listen to millions of songs, podcasts, and playlists across devices.

Yes, Spotify offers a free version with ads. Users can upgrade to Spotify Premium for ad-free listening, offline downloads, and high-quality audio.

Yes, with a Spotify Premium subscription, users can download songs, albums, and podcasts to listen offline without using data.

Spotify is available on smartphones, tablets, desktop computers, smart TVs, gaming consoles, and smart speakers like Alexa or Google Home.

Spotify offers different Premium plans such as Individual, Duo, Family, and Student. Prices vary by country; check the Spotify website or app for current rates.

Yes, Spotify allows users to create, edit, and share custom playlists. You can add songs from Spotify’s vast music library to your playlists anytime.
